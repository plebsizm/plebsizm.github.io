FAQ

Thank you for checking this page out! As a quick rundown, MICA Team have been releasing tons of merchandise in the Mainland China region throughout the years, and just recently have they been able to collaborate with companies on distributing very specific goods globally.
What about all the other ones though? The 'global merch store' hope has existed ever since the game has been out, but the accessibility of the merchandise is very limited for any player in regions outside of China. Until the dream of globalized merchandise becomes reality, I decided to make a small catalogue with various items that are offered on their taobao/tmall site! Below is a quick FAQ on the current functions of the site, and of course, a way on how you could get merchandise yourself!

1: Is this official?
- This catalogue is unofficial, but the merchandise itself is by MICA's affiliated company, and the displayed listings are the official ones in tmall/taobao.

2: The link forces me to log in. | The listing is incomplete. How do I preview them fully?
- There are various taobao agents like Superbuy and CSSBuy which allow you to paste the selected link and preview the entirety of the item listing, no login should be required. I personally use Superbuy.

3: Why are the links not clickable?
- The purpose of this site is to be as simple and straightforward as possible. This means - no clickable hyperlinks, no scripts. Since it's unofficial, though, it is best to not have any automation present.

4: The status of item 'X' is incorrect, it is sold out. | There's merchandise not present on this site, when will it be added?
- Each item in this site is manually imported, so apologies if there's any conflicting information. This site will always have a 'Last Updated' at the top, indicating its relevancy.

5: Can I purchase items for myself? Can it be delivered to my address?
- Yes! However... Taobao sellers offer shipping to a very limited amount of countries, and accessing the site directly requires registering on their platform. Registering through mobile is easier, but it requires a phone number.
